Our Research Focus

Prof. Konwar's laboratory focuses on the development of novel cancer nanotechnologies, therapeutics, and imaging methods. The lab is also actively involved in the discovery of potential therapeutic molecules from the rich bioresources of North-East India.

The major goal is to address key challenges in oncology — early cancer detection, targeted drug delivery, and non-invasive assessment of therapeutic response.

Core Research Themes

Cancer Nanotechnology

Development of novel nanotechnology-based approaches for cancer diagnosis, therapy, and biomedical applications including nanoparticle synthesis and surface functionalisation.

Nanoparticles Theranostics Bioconjugation

Therapeutic Development

Design and evaluation of novel therapeutic strategies — including small molecules, biologics, and combination approaches — to improve cancer treatment outcomes.

Small Molecules Combination Therapy In Vivo Models

Cancer Imaging & Diagnostics

Development and application of advanced imaging methods for early cancer detection and non-invasive monitoring of therapeutic response in preclinical models.

Fluorescence MRI Contrast Non-invasive Therapeutic Monitoring

North-East India Bioresources

Discovery and evaluation of bioactive natural products and anticancer molecules from the rich biodiversity of North-East India.

Early Cancer Detection

Biomarkers Diagnostics Point-of-Care

Suggested Workflow
Integrated Cancer Theranostic Pipeline

From bioresource discovery to clinical translation — a four-stage pipeline that defines how research moves from concept to impact.

01
Discovery
Biomarkers & Natural Products
Identification
Identification of cancer biomarkers and bioactive molecules from North-East Indian bioresources through molecular screening and ethnopharmacological leads.
02
Design
Theranostics & Nanotechnology
Development
Development of nanoparticles, imaging probes, and targeted therapeutic systems — engineered for specificity, bioavailability, and multimodal function.
03
Evaluation
Preclinical Validation
In Vitro & In Vivo
Rigorous in vitro and in vivo evaluation of efficacy, safety, and mechanistic understanding using clinically relevant cancer models.
04
Translation
Clinical Relevance
Bench to Bedside
Advancing validated technologies toward diagnostic and therapeutic applications with real-world patient impact through clinical translation.

Esha Receives Best Poster Presentation Award at NSARA
Esha has been awarded the Best Poster Presentation at the National Symposium and Workshop on Crosstalk between Animal Research & Alternatives (NSARA), organised by CSIR-NEIST. Her outstanding contribution to the symposium reflects the high calibre of research carried out at the Integrated Cancer Theranostic Laboratory.
Congratulations, Esha!

Lydia Receives DST-INSPIRE Fellow Award & Joins University of Delhi as JRF
Lydia received her DST-INSPIRE Fellow Award Letter and has joined as a Junior Research Fellow (JRF) at the University of Delhi, India, under the supervision of Dr. Alo Nag, Department of Biochemistry. We are immensely proud of her achievement and wish her a wonderful and fulfilling journey ahead.
Best wishes, Lydia!

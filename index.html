About the Lab
Our Vision & Mission

The Integrated Cancer Theranostic Laboratory is committed to bridging the gap between cancer diagnostics and therapeutics. Our multidisciplinary team works at the intersection of molecular biology, medicinal chemistry, nanotechnology, and clinical translation — with the goal of developing next-generation tools for early cancer detection and effective, targeted treatment.

We believe in science that matters — translating laboratory discoveries into real-world impact for patients. Our lab fosters a collaborative, rigorous, and creative research environment where students and scientists grow together.

Molecular Oncology

Decoding cancer biology at the molecular level to identify novel therapeutic targets.

Drug Discovery

Designing and screening small-molecule and natural-product-based anticancer agents.

Nanotechnology

Engineering nanoformulations for targeted drug delivery and enhanced bioavailability.

Clinical Translation

Bridging bench-to-bedside by validating discoveries in clinically relevant models.
